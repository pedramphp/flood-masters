Things to Do Before We Arrive

  • If possible, identify and eliminate the source.
  • If the power is out, contact your local provider and let us know immediately.
  • Schedule a plumber if necessary so we can begin immediately upon arrival.
  • Remove valuables from the area if it is a safe environment.
  • Begin documenting by taking photos and writing notes.
